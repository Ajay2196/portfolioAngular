Reflection

AJ’s BookMented Reality Application helped me revisit my software engineering days, but however with a perspective of a budding interaction designer, I was able to focus more onto the interaction of the application, I was able to model and structure the application accordingly. I had started the whole process with the storyboarding and modelling the flow of the application and then entered the application development. When I say that it made me revisit my software engineering days, I realised how I missed debugging applications, getting to the root of the application’s issues as I faced many issues and tried various different approaches to resolve the many issues that I had encountered. But eventually emerging with a working solution gave me the satisfaction I thrive on.
